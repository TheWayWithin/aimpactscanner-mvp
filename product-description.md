**Version**: v1.0

## Product Overview

**AImpactScanner** is a professional-grade SaaS platform that delivers instant AI optimization analysis for any website. Built on the proven MASTERY-AI Framework v3.1.1, we provide enterprise-quality insights in under 15 seconds, helping businesses unlock their AI potential without the complexity or cost of traditional consultancy.

### Value Proposition

**"Professional AI optimization analysis in under 15 seconds – no consultants required."**

- **Speed**: Sub-15 second analysis vs. weeks of traditional consulting
- **Accuracy**: 500%+ accuracy improvement through systematic validation
- **Accessibility**: Self-service platform vs. $10k+ consulting engagements
- **Actionability**: Specific, implementable recommendations with educational context
