Today I removed an invisible ceiling from my product.

Supabase Edge Functions have a 60-second timeout. Sounds fine until you realize:

- React SPAs need headless browsers to analyze
- Headless browsers need 90-120 seconds to render complex sites
- My target market builds... React SPAs

I was building an AI visibility scanner that couldn't analyze my target customers' websites.

The fix? Migrating from Edge Functions to Railway Node.js.

No more timeout limits.
Full Puppeteer support coming.
React, Vue, Angular - all analyzable.

The migration wasn't trivial:
- New Express backend with full middleware stack
- Async job queue for long-running analyses
- PostgreSQL-based worker coordination
- Feature flags for gradual rollout

First production analysis completed today: stackoverflow.com, 73/100 score, 19 factors.

Sometimes the most important work is invisible.

No user cares about my infrastructure. But they WILL care when I can finally analyze their React app.
