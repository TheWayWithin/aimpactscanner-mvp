## Issues Resolved

### Issue: Analysis Timeout After Sprint 3 Deployment
- **Symptom**: All analyses timing out after adding SEO factors
- **Root Cause**: `analyzeInternalLinking` regex causing catastrophic backtracking on pages with many links
- **Fix**: Two-step regex approach with MAX_LINKS=500 limit, reduced delays, increased timeout
- **Prevention**: Test with link-heavy pages before deploying regex changes

---

## Lessons Learned

1. **Regex can be dangerous** - A simple anchor tag regex caused 30+ second hangs on link-heavy pages
2. **Doug Hall framework works** - Identifying the "dramatic difference" (2-in-1 AI + SEO tool) immediately clarified messaging
3. **Jargon kills conversion** - "Phase A/B" means nothing to customers; "AI readiness + SEO foundation" communicates real value
