## Value Chain: Free → Solo → Growth → Scale

The pricing ladder is designed to move users up through **demonstrated value** — each tier unlocks capabilities that the previous tier deliberately withholds, creating natural upgrade triggers.

```

```

## Tier Breakdown

### FREE — The Hook

**Price**: $0 forever

**What you get**:
- 3 analyses per month
- 18-factor MASTERY-AI analysis across 8 pillars
- 9 foundational SEO checks (indexability, mobile, speed, etc.)
- Basic AI recommendations
- Watermarked report downloads
- Standard analysis speed
- Community support
- Web dashboard access

**Purpose in the value chain**: Let users experience the product with zero commitment. The 3-scan limit creates natural friction — enough to see the value, not enough to rely on it. Watermarked reports prevent using Free for professional work.

**Upgrade trigger**: Users hit the 3-scan ceiling quickly. Watermarked reports look unprofessional if shared with clients or stakeholders.

## Annual vs Monthly Incentive Strategy

### The Annual Discount Structure

| Tier | Monthly Cost | Annual Cost | Monthly Equivalent | Savings | Savings % |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Solo | $5.95 | $49.50 | $4.13 | $21.90/yr | 31% |
| Growth | $17.95 | $149.50 | $12.46 | $65.90/yr | 31% |
| Scale | $34.95 | $299.50 | $24.96 | $119.90/yr | 29% |

### Why Annual Is Better for Everyone

**For the user**:
- ~30% savings across all tiers
- Growth annual includes a **7-day free trial** (monthly doesn't)
- Lock in current pricing before increases
- No monthly billing friction

**For the business**:
- 12 months of committed revenue upfront
- Dramatically lower churn (annual churn ~5-10% vs monthly ~8-15%)
- Better cash flow forecasting
- Reduced payment processing overhead
- Higher LTV per customer

### Annual Incentive Tactics (Implemented)

1. **Visual savings badges** — Every tier card shows "Save $X/year" prominently
2. **Monthly equivalent display** — Annual price shown as "$X.XX/mo" to make comparison easy
3. **Default to annual** — Billing toggle defaults to annual view
4. **Trial exclusivity** — Growth 7-day free trial only available on annual billing
5. **Urgency messaging** — "Price increases next week" countdown timer
6. **Social proof** — Live visitor counter showing active users

## Value Ladder Psychology

### The Anchoring Effect

Scale at $34.95/mo makes Growth at $17.95/mo feel like a bargain. Growth is deliberately positioned as "MOST POPULAR" because:

1. It's roughly **half the price of Scale** — feels like a deal
2. It has **10x the features of Solo** — massive perceived value jump
3. The **7-day trial on annual** eliminates risk entirely
4. It's the tier where the product becomes **genuinely useful** (remediation planner, LLM.txt generation, competitor benchmarking)

### The "Missing Middle" Strategy

Solo is intentionally limited. It exists to:
1. Get the first dollar (hardest conversion)
2. Demonstrate value through educational insights
3. Create frustration via missing features (no full analysis, no remediation planner)
4. Make Growth the obvious next step

Solo users who are serious about AI readiness will naturally upgrade to Growth within 1-2 months because Solo gives them **awareness without action** — they know their scores but can't fix them efficiently.

### The Lock-In at Scale

Scale users build AImpactScanner into their business operations through API and white-label. This creates:
- **Technical lock-in**: API integrations are expensive to replace
- **Client lock-in**: White-label reports mean their clients expect the format
- **Workflow lock-in**: Webhooks and automations depend on the platform
- **Relationship lock-in**: Dedicated account manager creates personal connection
