## Getting Started

### What is AImpactScanner?

AImpactScanner helps you optimize your website for AI search engines and LLM visibility. Using the MASTERY-AI Framework, we analyze your site and provide actionable recommendations to improve your AI discoverability.

### New Authentication System

We've upgraded to a modern, passwordless authentication system that offers:
- **No passwords to remember** - Sign in with Google, GitHub, or magic link
- **Instant access** - Get started in seconds
- **Secure** - OAuth providers handle your security
- **Seamless** - Your journey is preserved across signup and payment

## Sign Up Methods

### Method 1: Google OAuth (Recommended)

**Best for**: Everyone - fastest and most convenient

1. Visit [aimpactscanner.com](https://aimpactscanner.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** button
3. Select your desired tier (Free, Coffee, Growth, Scale)
4. Click **"Continue with Google"** button
5. Choose your Google account
6. Grant permissions (email, profile)
7. You're in! ✅

**What happens next**:
- Free tier: Go directly to analysis page
- Coffee tier: Complete payment, then access analysis
- Growth/Scale tier: Join waitlist

**Privacy**: We only request your email and name. No access to your Google data.

---

### Method 2: GitHub OAuth

**Best for**: Developers and GitHub users

1. Visit [aimpactscanner.com](https://aimpactscanner.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** button
3. Select your desired tier
4. Click **"Continue with GitHub"** button
5. Authorize AImpactScanner
6. You're in! ✅

**What happens next**: Same as Google OAuth based on selected tier

**Privacy**: We only request your email. No access to your repositories.

---

### Method 3: Magic Link (Email)

**Best for**: Users who prefer not to use OAuth

1. Visit [aimpactscanner.com](https://aimpactscanner.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** button
3. Select your desired tier
4. Click **"Continue with Email"** button
5. Enter your email address
6. Click **"Send Magic Link"**
7. Check your email inbox
8. Click the magic link (expires in 1 hour)
9. You're in! ✅

**Email delivery time**: Usually under 30 seconds

**Didn't receive the email?**
- Check your spam folder
- Click "Resend Magic Link"
- Wait a full minute before retrying
- Contact support@aimpactscanner.com

## Troubleshooting

### Authentication Issues

#### Problem: "Invalid credentials" error

**For OAuth users**:
- Try different OAuth provider (Google vs GitHub)
- Clear browser cookies and try again
- Ensure you're using the same email address

**For Magic Link users**:
- Check spelling of email address
- Wait full 30 seconds for email delivery
- Check spam folder
- Click "Resend Magic Link"

---

#### Problem: OAuth popup blocked

**Solution**:
1. Enable popups for aimpactscanner.com in browser settings
2. Try again
3. Alternatively, use Magic Link method

**Chrome**:
- Click popup icon in address bar
- Select "Always allow popups from aimpactscanner.com"

**Firefox**:
- Click blocked popup notification
- Select "Allow popups for aimpactscanner.com"

---

#### Problem: Magic Link expired

**Why this happens**: Magic links expire after 1 hour for security

**Solution**:
1. Return to login page
2. Enter your email again
3. Click "Send Magic Link"
4. Use new link within 1 hour

**Tip**: Click the link as soon as you receive the email.

---

### Payment Issues

#### Problem: Payment declined

**Common reasons**:
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired
- Incorrect billing address
- Bank fraud protection triggered

**Solution**:
1. Check card details in Stripe checkout
2. Contact your bank if declined repeatedly
3. Try different payment method
4. Contact support@aimpactscanner.com

**Your Free tier still works**: Payment failure doesn't block your account.

---

#### Problem: Payment succeeded but tier not updated

**Usually resolves in**: 5-30 seconds (webhook processing)

**If still not updated after 2 minutes**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Log out and log back in
3. Check Account Dashboard for subscription status
4. If still showing Free tier, contact support with:
   - Your email address
   - Approximate payment time
   - Stripe confirmation email

**We'll fix it manually**: Support team can verify payment and upgrade your account.

---

#### Problem: Charged twice

**This is rare but here's what to do**:
1. Check your Stripe receipt emails (you'll receive one per charge)
2. Contact support@aimpactscanner.com immediately
3. Include both charge IDs
4. We'll investigate and refund duplicate charge within 1 business day

**Our policy**: We never charge twice. Duplicates are always refunded immediately.

---

### Analysis Issues

#### Problem: URL context not preserved after signup

**What this means**: You entered URL on landing page but it's not pre-filled after signup

**Why this happens**:
- localStorage disabled (privacy mode)
- Different browser/device used for signup
- Context expired (older than 24 hours)

**Solution**:
- Simply enter URL again in analysis page
- Your analysis history is still saved

**For future**: Use same browser for landing page and signup.

---

#### Problem: Can't export PDF (Free tier)

**This is expected**: PDF export is a Coffee tier feature

**Solution**:
1. Upgrade to Coffee tier ($4.95/month)
2. Instant access to PDF exports
3. Unlimited analyses included

**Why upgrade?**:
- Professional reports for clients
- Shareable insights
- Unlimited analyses

---

### Account Issues

#### Problem: Locked out of account

**Solution**:
1. Try all authentication methods you've used:
   - Google OAuth
   - GitHub OAuth
   - Magic Link
2. Check email for magic link if you requested one recently
3. Contact support@aimpactscanner.com with your email

**We can help**: Support team can verify your identity and restore access.

---

#### Problem: Wrong tier shown

**Check first**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Log out and log back in
3. Check Stripe Customer Portal for subscription status

**If still incorrect**:
- Contact support@aimpactscanner.com
- Include: Expected tier, Current tier shown, Stripe receipt (if applicable)
- We'll correct it within 1 business day

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is AImpactScanner safe to use?**
A: Yes! We use industry-standard security:
- OAuth authentication (Google, GitHub)
- All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- Stripe for payment processing (PCI compliant)
- No passwords stored (passwordless auth)
- Regular security audits

---

**Q: Do you store my website data?**
A: We store only the analysis results (scores, recommendations). We do not crawl or store your entire website content. Your data is protected by row-level security policies.

---

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes! Contact support@aimpactscanner.com and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 7 business days per GDPR requirements.

---

### Authentication Questions

**Q: Can I use multiple authentication methods?**
A: Yes! If you sign up with Google, you can later use GitHub or Magic Link with the same email address. They'll all access the same account.

---

**Q: What if I change my Google/GitHub email?**
A: Contact support@aimpactscanner.com before changing. We can help migrate your account to a new email address.

**Q: Can I switch from Magic Link to OAuth?**
