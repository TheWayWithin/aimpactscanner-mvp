## Executive Summary

AImpactScanner is a diagnostic platform that evaluates individual web pages against the 27-factor MASTERY-AI Framework and tells practitioners exactly where they stand in AI search, what to fix, and whether their fixes worked.

It operates within the AI Search Mastery branded house ecosystem as the diagnostic engine. LLM.txt Mastery provides the optimization engine. AISearchArena provides independent tool benchmarking. Together, the three properties form a complete practitioner workflow: Diagnose, Optimize, Evaluate.

**Strategic Position:** Page-level AI search readiness diagnostic tool
**Market:** Solopreneurs, content creators, and small agencies navigating AI search visibility
**Differentiation:** 27-factor MASTERY-AI Framework analysis at accessible pricing ($0-$39.95/month), integrated with optimization tooling
**Business Model:** Tiered freemium-to-paid with ecosystem integration at Growth and Scale tiers
**Brand Architecture:** Branded house product under AI Search Mastery (parent hub)

## 1. Vision Statement

> **Every practitioner knows exactly how AI-ready their content is, what to fix first, and whether their fixes worked.**

AI search optimization should not be a black box. The practitioners we serve -- solopreneurs running businesses, consultants advising clients, small agencies managing multiple sites -- should be able to answer three questions about any page they publish:

1. How visible is this page to AI search engines right now?
2. What specific factors are holding it back?
3. Did the changes I made actually improve things?

AImpactScanner exists to make those three questions answerable in under 15 seconds, at a price that makes checking routine rather than exceptional.

---

## 2. Mission Statement

> **Every month, we help practitioners diagnose their AI search readiness against standardized criteria, prioritize what to fix, and measure whether it worked.**

Three commitments embedded in this mission:

- **Standardized criteria** -- The MASTERY-AI Framework provides consistent, repeatable analysis. Pages are evaluated against the same 27 factors every time, so results are comparable across scans and over time.

- **Prioritize what to fix** -- A score without a next step is a vanity metric. Every analysis identifies the highest-impact gaps and recommends specific actions.

- **Measure whether it worked** -- The Diagnose-Optimize-Test-Repeat loop closes the feedback cycle. Practitioners do not have to guess whether their changes helped. They re-scan and see.

---

## 3. Core Purpose (Timeless)

> **To give practitioners an honest, actionable picture of their AI search readiness -- so optimization decisions are based on evidence, not guesswork.**

This purpose does not expire. Whether AI search is called GEO, AEO, or something coined next year, whether the MASTERY-AI Framework has 27 factors or 270, the need for clear diagnostics persists. Practitioners will always need to know where they stand before they can improve.

**Why this matters now:** AI-powered search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini) is becoming a primary discovery channel. Most practitioners do not know how their content performs in this context. The tools that exist are either enterprise-priced ($99-$5,000/month), narrowly scoped, or inconsistent in methodology. There is a gap between "AI search matters" (which most practitioners now accept) and "here is exactly what to do about it" (which most practitioners still lack). AImpactScanner fills that gap at the page level.

## 4. Brand Essence and Values

### Brand Essence

The parent brand essence is **"Clarity."** AImpactScanner's expression of clarity is diagnostic precision -- turning the abstract question "is my content AI-ready?" into specific, scored, actionable answers.

Clarity in a diagnostic context means:
- Scores trace back to defined factors, not opaque algorithms
- Recommendations are specific enough to act on within 24 hours
- Progress is measurable by re-scanning after changes
- Limitations are stated honestly (what the tool does not cover is as clear as what it does)

### How Parent Brand Values Express Through AImpactScanner

**1. Clarity Over Cleverness**

If a recommendation cannot be explained plainly, it does not belong in the report. Every MASTERY-AI factor score includes an explanation of what was measured and why it matters. No jargon without definition. No scores without context.

**Behavioral test:** Can a solopreneur with no SEO background understand their report and identify their top three priorities without external help?

**2. Honesty Over Hype**

AImpactScanner does not manufacture urgency. It does not claim to guarantee AI search rankings. It diagnoses readiness and recommends improvements -- that is the honest scope. When the tool cannot assess something reliably, it says so.

**Behavioral test:** Does every claim on the marketing site and in the product hold up under scrutiny from a skeptical practitioner?

**3. Action Over Analysis**

Every scan ends with prioritized next steps. The product is not a dashboard to contemplate -- it is a diagnostic that leads to action. The Diagnose-Optimize-Test-Repeat loop exists because action without measurement is guessing, and measurement without action is procrastination.

**Behavioral test:** Can a user take their first optimization action within 30 minutes of receiving their report?

**4. Accessibility Is The Mission**

Professional AI search diagnostics should not require an enterprise budget. Pricing starts at $0 (3 scans/month, no credit card). Paid tiers start at $9.95/month. The 3 Gaps Guarantee on paid tiers means practitioners risk nothing by trying. The product works on a phone at 9pm as well as a desktop at 2pm.

**Behavioral test:** Can a solopreneur earning $50K/year justify this expense without hesitation?

**5. Build To Evolve**

The MASTERY-AI Framework started at 18 factors and now covers 27. It will continue to evolve as AI search technology changes. The product architecture supports this evolution without breaking existing workflows or invalidating historical data.

**Behavioral test:** Can we add new factors to the framework without requiring users to learn a new interface or lose their scan history?

## 8. Product Overview

### The MASTERY-AI Framework

The MASTERY-AI Framework is a structured evaluation methodology for AI search readiness. It currently covers **27 factors** organized across 8 pillars:

| Pillar | Focus Area |
|--------|-----------|
| **M** - Machine Readability | Technical accessibility for AI crawlers |
| **A** - Authority | Credibility signals and trust indicators |
| **S** - Semantic | Content structure and meaning clarity |
| **T** - Topical | Subject depth and expertise signals |
| **E** - Engagement | Content quality and user value indicators |
| **R** - Reference | Citation worthiness and source quality |
| **Y** - Yield | Actionable output and practical value |
| **AI** - AI-Specific | Factors unique to AI search engine behavior |

The framework evolves as AI search technology changes. Factor count has grown from 18 (launch) to 27 (current). This evolution is documented and versioned so historical comparisons remain meaningful.

### The Diagnose-Optimize-Test-Repeat Loop

This is the core product workflow:

1. **DIAGNOSE** -- Scan a page against 27 MASTERY-AI factors. Receive a score, gap analysis, and prioritized recommendations.
2. **OPTIMIZE** -- Implement recommended changes. At Growth and Scale tiers, LLM.txt Mastery is included to address llms.txt-related gaps directly.
3. **TEST** -- Re-scan the same page. Compare before and after scores. See which factors improved and which still need work.
4. **REPEAT** -- Continue the cycle. Track historical progress. Compound improvements over time.

The loop is what distinguishes AImpactScanner from a one-time audit tool. The value grows with repeated use because practitioners can measure their optimization progress quantitatively.

## 9. Competitive Landscape

### Primary Alternative: Manual AI Analysis (Free)

Practitioners paste URLs into ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity and ask for an analysis.

**Why they choose it:** Free, instant, no commitment.
**Why AImpactScanner wins:** Framework consistency (27 factors, same methodology every time), historical tracking, prioritized recommendations, the ability to measure improvement over time. Manual analysis produces different results depending on the prompt, the model version, and the day. AImpactScanner produces comparable, repeatable scores.

### Direct Competitors

The GEO/AEO tool market is early-stage and fragmented. Competitors include platforms like Ayzeo ($15/month, 10,000+ websites analyzed) and emerging tools on waitlists. The competitive landscape changes monthly.

**Why AImpactScanner wins:** The MASTERY-AI Framework (27 factors, documented methodology), ecosystem integration with LLM.txt Mastery (Diagnose-Optimize-Test-Repeat loop), and accessible pricing with the 3 Gaps Guarantee.

**Honest acknowledgment:** This is a new market. Competitors are building quickly. Our advantage is not permanent -- it must be maintained through framework evolution, product quality, and ecosystem value. AISearchArena's monthly benchmarks provide market intelligence that helps us stay informed about competitive movement.

### Indirect Competitors: Enterprise Brand Monitoring

Platforms like Profound ($99+/month) and AthenaHQ ($295+/month) offer brand-level AI visibility tracking.

**Why they are not direct competitors:** They serve enterprise teams with enterprise budgets. They monitor brand visibility across AI platforms. AImpactScanner diagnoses individual pages for solopreneurs. Different scope, different audience, different price point.

### Substitutes: Traditional SEO Tools

Yoast, RankMath, Semrush, and similar tools optimize for traditional search engines.

**Why AImpactScanner is different:** AI search engines evaluate content differently from Google's crawler. Factors like citation worthiness, semantic structure for LLM comprehension, and llms.txt optimization are not addressed by traditional SEO tools. AImpactScanner is AI-native -- built specifically for how AI search engines discover and surface content.

### Competitive Moat

The moat is built through compounding advantages:

1. **Framework depth** -- 27 factors, evolved from 18, continuing to grow. Replicating this requires sustained research into AI search engine behavior.
2. **Ecosystem integration** -- The Diagnose-Optimize-Test-Repeat loop with LLM.txt Mastery creates a workflow that standalone tools cannot match without building their own optimization engine.
3. **Pricing accessibility** -- Bootstrap economics allow profitable operation at price points that enterprise-focused competitors cannot profitably serve.
4. **Practitioner trust** -- The 3 Gaps Guarantee, transparent methodology, and honest communication compound into trust that takes time to build and is difficult to shortcut.

### Revenue Philosophy

**1. Value before gate.** The Free tier provides 3 genuine scans per month. The product proves its value before asking for money. Watermarked reports at Free tier prevent professional use but do not degrade the diagnostic insight.

**2. Growth as the gravity well.** The tier structure is designed so Growth ($19.95/month) is the natural home for serious practitioners. It includes LLM.txt Mastery, completing the Diagnose-Optimize-Test-Repeat loop. The pricing relationship between tiers makes Growth the obvious choice for anyone who found value in their first few scans.

**3. Transparency as trust.** Pricing is published, permanent, and identical for all customers. No hidden fees. No countdown timers. No "limited time" offers. The 25% annual discount is the only pricing incentive, and it is always available.

**4. Risk reversal is genuine.** The 3 Gaps Guarantee on paid tiers is a real commitment: if the first paid scan does not reveal at least 3 specific gaps the user did not know about, full refund within 30 days. This guarantee works because the product reliably delivers on it.

**5. Ecosystem integration earns its place.** LLM.txt Mastery is included at Growth and Scale not as a marketing tactic but because the diagnostic loop requires it. The pricing reflects the combined value of both tools at those tiers.
