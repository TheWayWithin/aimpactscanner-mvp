# AImpactScanner Business Development Plan

## Executive Summary

This business development plan outlines the non-technical growth strategy for AImpactScanner, focusing on community-driven adoption, content-led marketing, and strategic positioning in the emerging AI optimization category. Designed for solopreneur execution with limited resources, the plan emphasizes high-leverage activities, automation, and authentic relationship building.

**Core Business Development Principles:**
- Build in public for trust and transparency
- Community before customers
- Education before sales
- Partnerships over paid advertising
- Sustainable growth over blitzscaling

## Market Positioning & Messaging

### Core Positioning Statement
"AImpactScanner is the AI optimization tool built by a solopreneur for solopreneurs, founders, and creators who want to understand and improve how AI sees their websites without the complexity of enterprise tools or the cost of consultants."

### Key Messages by Persona

#### Tech-Savvy Solopreneurs
- **Hook:** "Your website is invisible to AI"
- **Value:** "See exactly what ChatGPT sees"
- **Differentiator:** "Built by someone who gets your constraints"
- **CTA:** "Get your free AI visibility score"

#### Bootstrap SaaS Founders
- **Hook:** "AI is how users find software now"
- **Value:** "Make your SaaS discoverable in AI search"
- **Differentiator:** "Grow without growing your burn rate"
- **CTA:** "Join 100+ founders optimizing for AI"

#### Digital Creators/Developers
- **Hook:** "Optimize all your sites at once"
- **Value:** "Manage your entire portfolio efficiently"
- **Differentiator:** "Bulk tools for multi-site managers"
- **CTA:** "Start with your first site free"
