**AImpactScanner** is a professional AI search optimization analysis platform that evaluates individual web pages against the comprehensive 18-factor MASTERY-AI Framework. **How it shows**:
- All 18 factors implemented across 8 pillars (not "simplified" versions)

- Free tier provides SOME actionable value (3 real analyses), not just a "trial preview"
- We recommend llmtxtmastery only when gaps justify it (not always upselling)

**Decision test**: "Can a solopreneur take action in the next 24 hours based on this analysis?"

**What it means**: We succeed when the whole AI Search Mastery ecosystem succeeds, not just AImpactScanner in isolation.

**Decision test**: "Does this decision strengthen the AI Search Mastery ecosystem, not just AImpactScanner metrics?"

### 5. Evidence-Based Pivoting

**What it means**: We commit to Month 6 honest assessment—continue, pivot, or sunset based on evidence, not wishful thinking.

**How it shows**:
- We track MRR by tier, conversion rates, churn, costs publicly (no vanity metrics)
- Month 6 review asks "should we continue?" not just "how do we grow?"
