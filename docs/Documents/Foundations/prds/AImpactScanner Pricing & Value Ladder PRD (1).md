### 5.3 Key Design Principles

**Visual Hierarchy**:
1. Annual toggle selected by default (larger, highlighted)
2. Growth tier highlighted with ⭐ badge and "Most Popular"
3. Per-month price shown first (even for annual)
4. Total annual price shown second
5. Savings shown prominently in green

**Psychological Anchoring**:
1. Annual prices shown first (lower per-month price)
2. Monthly prices shown as "or $17.95/mo monthly" (secondary)
3. Savings quantified: "Save $65.90" (concrete benefit)

**Flexibility Messaging**:
1. "Cancel anytime" (reduces risk)
2. "Switch plans anytime" (reduces wrong-choice anxiety)
3. "Downgrade anytime" (acknowledges changing needs)

## 6. Messaging Framework

### 6.1 Doug Hall Marketing Physics Compliance

**Law 1: Overt Benefit (What's In It For Me?)**

Test: Can a 10-year-old understand the benefit in 5 seconds?

✅ **Solo**: "Stop guessing - analyze your 10 most important pages for less than a coffee"
✅ **Growth**: "Analyze 40 pages, track improvements for 90 days, help ChatGPT find your content"
✅ **Scale**: "Analyze 100 pages, keep data forever, automate with API"

**Law 2: Real Reason to Believe (Why Should I Trust This?)**

Test: Is there concrete, verifiable proof?

✅ **Research-based framework**: 18 factors proven by thousands of analyses
✅ **Correctly weighted**: Citation content 23.8% vs Page speed 3.2%
✅ **No LLM hallucination**: Same analysis every time, no randomness
✅ **Measurable speed**: 12 seconds vs 5+ minutes (25x faster)
✅ **Concrete cost**: $0.41 per analysis (annual) vs $5-10 manual

**Law 3: Dramatic Difference (10x Better Than Alternatives)**

Test: Is it 10x better in a way that matters to the customer?

✅ **25x faster**: 12 seconds vs 5 minutes
✅ **10-20x cheaper**: $12.46/mo vs $100-300 SEO tools
✅ **Systematic vs chaos**: Consistent 18-factor framework vs random blog advice
✅ **Proven vs guesswork**: Research-based vs inventing your own checklist

### 6.2 Messaging Do's and Don'ts

**DO Use This Language**:

Framework Benefits:
- ✅ "Research-based framework, not guesswork"
- ✅ "Correctly weighted - know what matters most"
- ✅ "No LLM hallucination or inconsistency"
- ✅ "Evidence-based recommendations from YOUR page"

Time & Cost:
- ✅ "12 seconds vs 5+ minutes"
- ✅ "$0.41 per analysis (annual) vs $5-10 manual"
- ✅ "25x faster than manual ChatGPT"
- ✅ "10x cheaper than expensive SEO tools"

Outcomes:
- ✅ "See your score improve from 65 to 82"
- ✅ "Know what to fix first - high-impact issues"
- ✅ "Catch problems during development, not after launch"
- ✅ "Help ChatGPT find your content"

**DON'T Use This Language**:

Avoid Jargon:
- ❌ "MASTERY-AI Framework v3.1.1" → ✅ "Research-based framework"
- ❌ "18-factor evaluation" → ✅ "18 proven factors"
- ❌ "Evidence-based scoring algorithm" → ✅ "Shows actual examples from your page"
- ❌ "AI-readable LLMS.txt" → ✅ "Help ChatGPT find your content"

Avoid Vague Claims:
- ❌ "Professional-grade analysis" → ✅ "Same framework free or paid"
- ❌ "Optimize your portfolio" → ✅ "Analyze 40 pages per month"
- ❌ "Save time" → ✅ "12 seconds vs 5+ minutes"
- ❌ "Affordable pricing" → ✅ "$0.41 per analysis"
