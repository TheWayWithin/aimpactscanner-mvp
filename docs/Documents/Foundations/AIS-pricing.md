## Pricing Philosophy

**Core principle**: Every tier delivers genuine value. Paid tiers are earned through demonstrated value, not manufactured limitation. The product proves itself before asking for money.

**Pricing Principles (from Unified Strategy):**

1. **Accessibility over extraction** -- Every tier is priced for the solopreneur and small business owner, not enterprise budgets.
2. **Value before gate** -- Free tiers deliver genuine insight. The product proves itself before asking for money.
3. **Transparency as trust** -- Our methodology is published. Our pricing is permanent. No countdown timers or expiring offers.
4. **Growth as gravity well** -- The entire value ladder is designed so Growth is the most natural tier for serious users.
5. **Loop completeness rewards commitment** -- Higher tiers complete the Diagnose-Optimize-Test-Repeat loop.

**Risk Reversal:**
- **No credit card required** for Free tier (zero friction entry)
- **30-day money-back guarantee** on all paid tiers (zero risk)
- **3 Gaps Guarantee:** If your first paid scan doesn't reveal at least 3 specific AI visibility gaps you didn't know about, full refund within 30 days

## Cross-Product Architecture

AImpactScanner is the **diagnostic engine** in the AI Search Mastery ecosystem. It pairs with LLM.txt Mastery (the optimization engine) to form the Diagnose-Optimize-Test-Repeat loop.

**Pricing relationship:** AImpactScanner is priced at exactly **2x** LLM.txt Mastery at every paid tier.

**Bundle integration:** At Growth ($19.95/mo) and Scale ($39.95/mo), AImpactScanner includes LLM.txt Mastery features **free** -- the diagnostic engine is the product at a fair price, and the optimization engine is included as a bonus. This is not a "bundle deal." It is the natural product architecture: the loop doesn't work without both tools.

```
AImpactScanner (Diagnose)  ->  Identifies gaps across 27 factors
        |
LLM.txt Mastery (Optimize) ->  Fixes the most common gap (llms.txt quality)
        |
AImpactScanner (Test)      ->  Re-scans to measure improvement
        |
        Repeat              ->  Compounds over time
```

## Upgrade Triggers

### Design Principles

1. **Value before gate** -- User must have experienced measurable value before seeing an upgrade prompt
2. **Behaviour-based, not time-based** -- Triggers activate on usage patterns, not arbitrary calendars
3. **Explicit permission to stay** -- Every message validates the current tier
4. **Incremental framing** -- Present price as delta from current spend, not absolute
5. **Specificity over superlatives** -- Name exact features gained, never "unlock premium features"
6. **No manufactured urgency** -- No countdown timers, no "limited spots," no expiring offers
7. **Math that checks out** -- Every percentage and comparison must be mathematically verifiable

### Trigger Messages

#### Free -> Solo ($0 -> $9.95/mo)

**Trigger:** User reaches 3rd free scan and attempts a 4th, or sees gated recommendations.

**Message:**
> "Your scan found [X] AI visibility gaps across [Y] of 27 factors. Solo ($9.95/mo) unlocks full diagnostic detail, clean PDF reports, and 10 scans/month. The free scan showed you where you stand. Solo shows you what to do about it."

#### Solo -> Growth ($9.95 -> $19.95/mo)

**Trigger:** User has improved their score across multiple cycles, or attempts Remediation Planner / competitor benchmarking.

**Message:**
> "Your AI visibility score improved from [X] to [Y] -- that's the Diagnose-Test loop working. Growth adds the Optimize step: LLM.txt Mastery Growth features included free, plus Remediation Planner and competitor benchmarking. All Growth-level diagnostic features at a fair price, plus the optimization engine (worth $9.95/mo) as a bonus. $19.95/mo total."

#### Growth -> Scale ($19.95 -> $39.95/mo)

**Trigger:** User manages multiple client sites, hits analysis limits, or needs API/white-label.

**Message:**
> "You're scanning [X] sites regularly. Scale includes API access, white-label reports, team collaboration, and LLM.txt Scale features free. At $39.95/mo with [X] sites, that's approximately $[per-site]/site/month. The nearest competitor with comparable depth starts at $99/mo for a single site."

### Cross-Product Triggers

#### Both Products at Solo -> AImpactScanner Growth

**Trigger:** System detects user paying LLM.txt Solo ($4.95) + AIS Solo ($9.95) = $14.90/mo separately.

**Message:**
> "You're currently paying $14.90/mo for LLM.txt Solo and AImpactScanner Solo separately. AImpactScanner Growth is $19.95/mo and includes LLM.txt Growth features -- an upgrade on both products for $5.05/mo more. One subscription instead of two, tighter integration, Growth-tier capability across both tools."

### In-App Upgrade Rules

- Maximum 1 upgrade prompt per session (don't nag)
- Always show what they GET, not what they DON'T have
- Include the price to reduce friction ("Just $9.95/mo")
- Show the guarantee ("30-day money-back, no questions asked")
- Never fire before the user has completed at least one successful analysis

## Objection Handling

### "It's too expensive"

**Response:** Start with the free tier. If the results show you something about how AI sees your site that you didn't already know -- and they almost always do -- you have your answer. Solo is $9.95/mo with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If one AI-referred client finds you through improved visibility, that pays for decades of the tool.

### "How is this different from Semrush?"

**Response:** Semrush tells you how Google sees your site. We tell you how ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini see you. Different engine, different signals, different tool. We're not a replacement -- we're the AI search layer Semrush doesn't cover. At $9.95-$39.95/mo vs their $99+, you're adding a focused tool for a fraction of what your existing platform costs.

### "I'm not sure AI search matters yet"

**Response:** Honest answer: it depends on your market. Some niches already see 20-30% of discovery moving to AI tools. Others aren't there yet. What we know is the trajectory. The free scan takes 15 seconds and costs nothing. At minimum, you'll know where you stand.

### "Why should I pay annually?"

**Response:** Monthly is completely fine. Same features both ways. Annual saves 25% (3 months free). Start monthly, switch to annual whenever you want. No deadline, no expiring offer. The 30-day guarantee applies to annual too.

### "What if it doesn't work for my site?"

**Response:** Works with any website that has a public URL -- WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Webflow, Framer, custom-built. Our Playwright-based rendering handles JS-heavy sites that basic crawlers see as blank pages. We can't promise specific AI recommendations (nobody honestly can), but we guarantee you'll discover at least 3 gaps you didn't know about, or full refund.

### "I don't understand the two products"

**Response:** 20-second explanation: AImpactScanner tells you what's wrong (27-factor diagnostic). LLM.txt Mastery fixes one of the most common things that's wrong (your llms.txt file). If you're starting from zero, LLM.txt Mastery is simpler and cheaper. If you want the full picture, AImpactScanner Growth includes LLM.txt free.

### "Why buy LLM.txt separately if AIS Growth includes it?"

**Response:** Good question. Two genuine reasons: (1) Not everyone needs the full diagnostic -- $4.95/mo solves the llms.txt problem specifically. (2) Some people want to start simple and cheap, then upgrade. But yes -- if you want both, AIS Growth at $19.95/mo is objectively the better deal.

### "Your competitors are more established"

**Response:** Semrush is excellent at traditional SEO. Cairrot is solid for agencies. We built everything from the ground up for AI search specifically. We move faster (no legacy code), publish our methodology (they hide theirs), and price for small business (they price for enterprise). Monthly subscription, cancel anytime, free tier to evaluate first.

### "I'll wait until the space matures"

**Response:** Reasonable position. AI visibility compounds -- businesses that optimise early build citation advantages that self-reinforce. The free scan doesn't expire. Neither does the 25% annual discount. We'll be here when the timing is right.
