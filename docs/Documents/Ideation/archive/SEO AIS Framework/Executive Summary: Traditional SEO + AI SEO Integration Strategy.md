## The Core Problem

Your intuition is correct: **AI SEO is fundamentally limited by basic SEO**. Our research confirms that AI Search Optimization is not a replacement for traditional SEO, but rather an advanced layer built on top of a solid traditional SEO foundation.

**Key Finding**: Without addressing foundational traditional SEO issues, AI optimization efforts deliver minimal results because:
- AI systems discover content through traditional search indexes
- AI trusts content that has traditional authority signals (backlinks, rankings)
- Technical SEO issues (broken links, poor site structure, slow speed) block AI access
- Content must rank in **top 10 traditional results** to be cited by AI
